## Radio 

I read and write news stories for the show *World View* on [WORT 89.9 FM](https://www.wortfm.org/). WORT is a local, community-run, non-profit radio station. *World View* is the oldest news show on WORT, and it features a half-hour round-up of the weekâ€™s international news, followed by a 20-30 minute feature, often a speech or in-depth interview. It is broadcasted on Sundays, 5 PM CST.
